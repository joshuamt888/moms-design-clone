WATER FEATURE INTEGRATION INTO YOUR DREAM LANDSCAPE DESIGN

In nature, water is soothing, yet powerful, reflective and restorative, and is downright essential! All creatures, especially humans, crave the sensory benefits that water affords. Adding a water feature to your landscaping offers numerous benefits, enhancing both aesthetics and functionality. They create a tranquil ambiance, providing soothing sounds that help reduce stress and drown out unwanted noise. A well-designed water feature serves as a stunning focal point, increasing visual appeal and value to your property.

Water features attract wildlife such as birds and butterflies, contributing a vibrant ecosystem to your landscape or garden. Additionally, water features can help regulate the temperature and humidity of your outdoor space, making it more comfortable and enjoyable throughout the year. 

What’s your pleasure; a pond that stirs blissful reminders of summer days at the lake, or a steam to mimic that rippling creek near your favorite fishing spot, or a sculptural fountain to create an artful focal point? Consider the possibilities!

TYPES OF WATER FEATURES

Fountains

A fountain is a decorative structure that circulates water, often creating a pleasant sound and visual appeal. Ideal for gardens and entrance points, fountains provide a calming atmosphere, attract wildlife, and serve as elegant focal points. 

Waterfalls

Waterfalls are a beautiful dynamic water feature that bring the sound and motion of cascading water to your outdoor space. These features create dramatic visual impact and are a captivating element to any landscape. From cascading or sheet waterfalls, to splash or wall waterfalls, the opportunities with this feature are endless. 

Bubbling Rocks

This feature offers a captivating yet simple way to incorporate a water feature into your space.  Bubbling rocks feature water that gently bubbles up from a natural or sculpted rock’s surface, creating and soothing, gentle stream. They add a touch of elegance with minimal maintenance required. Their subtle flow and tranquil sound make them ideal for enhancing outdoor spaces with a natural, peaceful ambiance. 

Water Walls

Water walls are a striking feature where water cascades down a vertical surface, creating a visually stunning effect. These walls can be constructed from various materials, including glass, stone, metal, and concrete. Glass water walls offer a modern, sleek appearance with a clear uninterrupted flow, whereas stone, metal, and concrete provide a more rustic and natural look.

Stream and Brook Features

These features mimic the sound and appearance of natural waterways, with meandering streams and gently babbling brooks that enhance the aesthetic appeal of any outdoor space. Carefully designed to fit your landscape, stream and brooks create a harmonious environment with the inclusions of rocks and plants. 

Swimming Pools with Integrated Water Elements

A swimming pool with integrated water features combines the functionality of a traditional swimming pool with the luxury and sensory appeal of features like waterfalls, fountains, and jets. These enhancements transform a standard pool into a more dynamic and enjoyable swimming experience. Investing in this feature not only increases the property’s value, but also provides a unique and engaging space for entertainment, relaxation, and exercise.

Bird Baths

Bird baths are a quaint feature to add to any landscape or garden. These charming additions draw in a wide range of birds, and can be made from a variety of materials, complementing any style of landscape architecture.

Aquatic Gardens

Aquatic gardens are enchanting water-based landscapes that bring the serene beauty of ponds, water plants, and aquatic life into residential or commercial spaces. These gardens can vary in size and complexity, from small container water gardens to extensive ponds integrated into the landscape.

Storm Water Solutions

Manage stormwater effectively with a custom-designed water feature that enhances both beauty and functionality in your landscape. Our expertly crafted rain gardens, retention ponds, and permeable water features help control runoff, reduce erosion, and replenish groundwater—all while creating a serene outdoor oasis.

Water Tables

Enhance your outdoor space with a custom water feature table, where the soothing flow of water meets functional design. Perfect for entertaining or creating a serene focal point, these unique tables integrate moving water into their surfaces, offering a mesmerizing blend of style and relaxation. Crafted for durability and beauty, our water tables can be tailored to complement any landscape or patio setting.
